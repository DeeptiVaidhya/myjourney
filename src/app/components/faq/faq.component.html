What is My Journey?

My Journey is a research study for breast cancer survivors. As part of the My Journey study, we have developed this website for women to use after they have undergone treatment for their breast cancer. We want to make sure patients have accurate and helpful information as they enter the survivorship phase.
What is the purpose of My Journey?

Through My Journey, we would like to learn more about the effects of endocrine therapy treatment on women diagnosed with breast cancer. Additionally, My Journey provides women with information about cancer treatments, symptom management, and tools to enhance coping and quality of life.
How can the My Journey study help me?

By participating in this study, you will have access to all of the information included in the My Journey website. Many of the resources in My Journey may be relevant to your breast cancer diagnosis.
Who has access to My Journey?

Only eligible participants who have enrolled in the My Journey study will have access to securely login to this website.
How many people will enroll in My Journey?

We expect about 80 people to enroll in My Journey.
How long will the research last?

If you enroll in the My Journey study, you will be asked to use the My Journey website for 8 weeks.

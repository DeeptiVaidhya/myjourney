What is OncoTool?

OncoTool is a research study for patients diagnosed with cancer. As a part of the OncoTool study, we have developed this website for patients to use while undergoing genomic tumor testing. We want to make sure patients have accurate and helpful information as they navigate the complex world of genomic tumor testing.
What is the purpose of OncoTool?

Through OncoTool, we hope to learn about patientsâ€™ concerns and needs throughout their experience with genomic tumor testing. Additionally, OncoTool provides patients with information about cancer treatments, symptom management, and tools to enhance coping and quality of life.
How can the OncoTool study help me?

By participating in this study, you will have access to all of the information included in the OncoTool website. Many of the resources in OncoTool may be relevant to your cancer treatment.
Who has access to OncoTool?

Only eligible patients who have enrolled in the OncoTool study will have access to securely login into this tool and website.
How many people will enroll in OncoTool?

We expect about 60 people to enroll in OncoTool.
How long will the research last?

If you enroll in the OncoTool study, you will be asked to use the OncoTool website for 8 weeks.

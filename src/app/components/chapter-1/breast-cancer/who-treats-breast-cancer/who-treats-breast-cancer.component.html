Who treats breast cancer?

Depending on the type of treatment you receive for breast cancer, you may have different types of doctors on your treatment team.

Common doctors on breast cancer treatment teams include:

  • A surgical oncologist or breast surgeon: a doctor who uses surgery to remove some or all of a breast cancer
  • A medical oncologist: a doctor who uses chemotherapy and other medicines to treat breast cancer and other cancers
  • A radiation oncologist a doctor who uses radiation to treat breast cancer and other cancers
  • A plastic surgeon: a doctor who specializes in reconstructing or repairing parts of the body.

Information in the My Journey website is provided for informational purposes only and is not intended as a substitute for the advice provided by your healthcare providers. The My Journey platform is not intended to be used for diagnosing or treating a health problem or disease. Information that we collect about you is used strictly for research and will only be shared with authorized members of the research team. It will not be shared with third parties. This is a HIPAA compliant website.

The only information that we will collect from you is your email address, your username information, your responses from your health questionnaires, activities that you complete on the website, and the amount of time that you spend on the website. We do not collect any information outside of the website activities previously mentioned.

Please note that the use of this program does not replace your standard of care by a physician. If you have an urgent medical or mental health issue, call 911 or go to the nearest emergency department.

Additionally, if you are using this website you are agreeing to participate in a research study. The use of My Journey is completely voluntary.

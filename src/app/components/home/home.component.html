What is OncoTool?

Tailored to You

OncoTool is an interactive website designed for people with advanced or metastatic cancer who are considering genomic tumor testing. Inside you will find information, videos, and resources, all of which were carefully chosen with you in mind.

Based on Research

All of the material in OncoTool is based on decades of research in cancer and cancer treatment. We sorted through the research, collected relevant and helpful information based on feedback from patients and oncology providers, and organized it all in one place.

Benefits of Participating

You will have access to all of the information, videos, and resources included in OncoTool. We hope OncoTool will help you understand genomic tumor testing, cancer treatments and possible side effects, and help you manage side effects and stress during this time.

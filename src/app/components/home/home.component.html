What is My Journey?

Tailored to You

My Journey is an interactive website designed for breast cancer survivors. Inside you will find information, videos, and resources, all of which were carefully chosen with you in mind.

Based on Research

All of the material in My Journey is based on research in breast cancer. We collected relevant information based on feedback from patients and oncology providers, and organized it all in one place.

Benefits of Participating

You will have access to all of the information, videos, and resources developed for breast cancer survivors. Our hope is that My Journey will improve the wellbeing of breast cancer survivors who have been prescribed a hormone therapy treatment.

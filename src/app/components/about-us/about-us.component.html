Welcome to My Journey!

We are a group of researchers at Northwestern University including psychologists, medical doctors, clinical counselors, and research assistants. Even though we have different training backgrounds, one thing we all have in common is our passion for providing the best care possible for patients with cancer. We combined our expertise to create My Journey, and to make sure that you have accurate and helpful information regarding your breast cancer diagnosis and endocrine therapy treatment. We hope that the information and tools in My Journey will help make your experience a little bit easier.

What does NOT cause breast cancer?

Here are the general recommendations for screening for breast cancer. Please remember, these may not apply to someone who has had breast cancer. Be sure to talk to your doctor about the best way to monitor your breast health moving forward.

Age Test Frequency
Women age 40-54 Mammography Once per year
Women age 55 and older Mammography Every other year
